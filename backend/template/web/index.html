Club Himalaya by ACE Hotels

Celebrates 25 Years of Exceptional Experiences

Batase Dandha, the barren Windy Hills of Nagarkot, was where the flower generation (so-called Hippies) pitched their tents, smoked wild-grown hashish, sang, and danced the whole night, waiting for the sunrise in the 60s and 70s. While the high-end tourists staying at The Royal Hotel, Soaltee or Annapurna woke up at 3:00 AM, drove for an hour and half over the rocky roads with elite breakfast boxes to reach this secluded Batase Dadha before 5:00 AM to catch a glimpse of those magical first rays on the Himalayan range.
At dawn, magic happens: the white snow peaks of Annapurna in the North West to Everest in the East, all turn to a golden persimmon hue. Had there only been some accommodation on this barren and chilly hillock, this experience would have been so much more comfortable.
